Baking for a living

The “Baking For A Living – Beyond Hobbies” courses are specifically designed to touch on the various aspects of baking and provide an entrepreneurial platform for our trainees. Each module is taught over 7 hours. Participants will be taught the fail-proof recipes and techniques of baking Pastries, Cakes, Breads and Cookies.


This series of courses are ideal for:

  • Bakers who have limited time to learn our Full Certification course
  • Individuals who wish to upgrade their baking skills
  • Bakers who wish to learn pointers on costings
  • Bakers who wish to learn how to get started on making 1st sale & learn tips on presentation skills
