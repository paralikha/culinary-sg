30 years

SSA Culinary Institute was founded in 2016 as another branch of the SSA Group of
Companies. With over 30 years of experience in the training and consultancy industry, SSA
Consulting Group now extends its training expertise in the culinary industry.

Renowned local
celebrity chef

Partnering with the renowned local celebrity chef, Siti Mastura, SSA Culinary
Institute promises a series of quality baking courses for its trainees – revolving
around personal development, entrepreneurship and teambuilding.

Accredited under
SkillsFuture and WSQ

Trainees to use their SkillsFuture Credit to pay for the baking courses. What’s more,
some of its courses are also WSQ-accredited which means trainees get to enjoy
subsidies! All ingredients used in our kitchen are halal.

Courses

Short Courses

The “Baking For A Living – Beyond Hobbies” courses are specifically designed to touch on the various aspects of baking and provide an entrepreneurial platform for our trainees.

WSQ Courses

Enjoy subsidies with WSQ-accredited courses which comprise a full competency map covering occupations in an industry with clear career development and training pathways.

CORPORATE TEAMBUILDING

Use our kitchen as a springboard for your organisation’s success! We provide your organisation with a great way to bond with fun and interactive team building experiences for you and your colleagues.

CUSTOMISED COURSES

Customise courses based on the needs of your organisation! Contact us for more details.

Children's Courses

Engage your child in a hands-on entrepreneurial experience!
